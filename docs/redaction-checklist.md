# Redaction Checklist

Use this checklist before publishing your own version of this repository.

## Remove Or Redact

- real patient or customer data
- real organization codes and identifiers
- legal or internal forms you are not allowed to redistribute
- internal network paths
- internal hostnames, usernames, tokens, or API keys
- screenshots containing protected information
- unreleased or proprietary business logic

## Safe To Share

- generalized workflow descriptions
- redacted field dictionaries
- synthetic sample rows
- architecture diagrams
- acceptance checklists
- reusable AI skills and instructions

## Final Review Questions

1. Can someone reconstruct protected source material from this repo?
2. Does any filename, screenshot, or example contain internal identifiers?
3. Are the instructions still useful after redaction?
4. Is the public repo focused on method rather than confidential implementation?
